Extract Images from PowerPoint

Arion Games provides paper minis for use on battle mats in two forms, PDF and PowerPoint (PPTX).

The problem? The images in the PDF are scaled to print about 25mm tall, too small compared to 32mm plastic minis. You can't just print the PDF with 25% zoom because the minis at the edge of the page will get clipped and they all get slightly blurrier.

One solution is to use the paper mini images in the PowerPoint files. You can copy and paste these to create your own sheets at whatever scale you'd like.

This extracts all of the images so you can scale them automatically, and upload them to your VTT.
